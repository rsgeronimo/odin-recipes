Description

To make a delicious pizza, start by preparing the dough. In a mixing bowl, combine 2 ½ cups of all-purpose flour, 2 teaspoons of instant yeast, 1 teaspoon of sugar, and 1 teaspoon of salt. Gradually add 1 cup of warm water and 2 tablespoons of olive oil to the dry ingredients, mixing until a soft dough forms. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Allow it to rise in a warm place for about an hour until it doubles in size. Once the dough has risen, preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out the dough into your desired pizza shape and thickness, then transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread a generous amount of pizza sauce over the dough and add your favorite toppings, such as mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Remove it from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and enjoy a delicious homemade pizza!

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by combining 2.5 cups of all-purpose flour, 2 teaspoons of instant yeast, 1 teaspoon of sugar, and 1 teaspoon of salt in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add 1 cup of warm water and 2 tablespoons of olive oil to the dry ingredients, and mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Allow the dough to rise in a warm place for about an hour until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475F (245C).
  6. Roll out the dough into your desired pizza shape and thickness, then transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  7. Spread a generous amount of pizza sauce over the dough.
  8. Add your favorite toppings, such as mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms.
  9. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
